My Goals

My journey in Computer Science isn’t only about skills, it’s about becoming the kind of engineer, leader, and person I want to be. These goals represent my long-term direction, purpose, and the future I’m working toward.

Become a Full-Stack Software Engineer

Become confident in modern full-stack technologies, including React, .NET/Node.js, and cloud platforms. My goal is to build scalable and user-focused applications that strengthen my technical foundation and prepare me for real-world software engineering challenges. My goal is to eventually design real applications that help people, not just projects that exist inside my laptop.

Deepen AI & ML Expertise

Continue learning advanced AI and machine learning concepts through hands-on projects, experimentation, and real data-driven applications. This will help me gain a deeper understanding of how intelligent systems are built and how they can be used to solve modern problems. I want to explore AI and Machine Learning not just as buzzwords, but as tools for solving real challenges—from healthcare, security, human-computer interaction, and more.

Build Impactful Tech for People

My long-term purpose in Computer Science is to use technology in a way that improves lives. Whether that’s through smart systems, data-driven insights, or automation, I want my work to have a positive impact and serve a purpose bigger than just writing code.

Continue Becoming My Best Self

Beyond just technical growth, I want to stay curious, keep learning, and continue building confidence in who I’m becoming as a young woman in tech. I want to lead, communicate well, believe in myself, and stay passionate about creating the future I dream of.

Where I See Myself

2026

Grow my skills during internships, build more advanced software, and learn from real engineering teams.

2027

Graduate and start a full-time role in software development or AI systems engineering.

2030

Lead projects that use technology for positive change in healthcare, education, accessibility, and social good.

My Long-Term Dream

Beyond titles or jobs, I want to become someone who uses technology with purpose, to help people, solve problems, and inspire others through my journey. My biggest dream is to grow into a woman in tech who leads with creativity, and impact.
