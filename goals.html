My Goals

These goals guide my growth as a software engineer by keeping me focused, accountable, and continuously improving.

Master Full-Stack Development

Become confident in modern full-stack technologies, including React, .NET/Node.js, and cloud platforms. My goal is to build scalable and user-focused applications that strengthen my technical foundation and prepare me for real-world software engineering challenges.

Deepen AI & ML Expertise

Continue learning advanced AI and machine learning concepts through hands-on projects, experimentation, and real data-driven applications. This will help me gain a deeper understanding of how intelligent systems are built and how they can be used to solve modern problems.

Contribute to Open Source

Explore new technologies and industry best practices by contributing to open-source projects. This will help sharpen my skills as a developer, expose me to collaborative coding environments, and expand my understanding of emerging tools and frameworks used in the tech community.

Strengthen Communication

Focus on improving how I present ideas, share updates, and document my work. By practicing clear communication and writing structured documentation, I aim to become a more confident and effective collaborator in team settings.
